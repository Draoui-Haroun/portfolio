My Journey

From Mechanical Engineering to Front-End Development.

before coding

My name is Haroun Draoui, and I am a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Science and Technology Houari Boumediene (USTHB).

For a long time, I was a typical student whose main goal was to achieve good grades and successfully complete academic requirements. However, deep down, I felt that I wanted more than just studying and passing exams. I wanted to develop valuable skills, explore new opportunities, and challenge myself beyond the classroom.

As I searched for a skill that I could learn alongside my university studies, I discovered graphic design. It was my first step into the world of digital creativity. Through graphic design, I learned important concepts about visual communication, aesthetics, and user-centered thinking—skills that continue to benefit me today.

How I Started Coding

The turning point in my journey came during my second year at university when we studied Python as part of the academic program. At first, it was just another course, but I quickly discovered that programming was a fascinating field full of challenges and opportunities for creativity. After the semester ended, I continued learning on my own, driven by curiosity and passion.

I deepened my understanding of Python by learning its fundamentals, data structures such as lists and dictionaries, and object-oriented programming (OOP) concepts including classes, inheritance, and other important principles. With every new concept I learned, my interest in programming continued to grow.

However, my curiosity did not stop with Python. It eventually led me to the world of web development, where I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Over time, I became particularly interested in Front-End Development because it combines creativity with technology and allows me to transform ideas into interactive and user-friendly web experiences.

Along this journey, I also realized the importance of the tools used by professional developers. As a result, I learned Git and GitHub to manage projects, track changes, and organize my development workflow effectively.

Today, I continue to improve my skills through continuous learning and hands-on projects, with a strong focus on Front-End Development and creating modern, responsive, and user-centered web interfaces.

My Mindset

My engineering background has shaped the way I think and work. It has taught me to analyze problems, seek efficient solutions, and remain persistent when facing challenges. I am driven by a mindset of continuous growth, always aiming to be better than I was yesterday.

I actively seek opportunities to learn, build, and improve my skills. Whether through academic studies, personal projects, or new technologies, I am committed to expanding my knowledge and pushing myself beyond my comfort zone to achieve meaningful growth.
